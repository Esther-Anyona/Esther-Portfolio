Mental Health Website

Over the past few years, the cases of mental health illnesses especially suicide deaths has been on the rise. This was heightened during the Covid-19 pandemic. A lack of mental wealth awareness has led to an increase in early non-detection of mental illnesses and associated challenges. This is the reason why a group of young professional therapists came up with an organization called MentalCheck, where they could offer their services. However, most people are unable to afford the high fees payable to mental health institutions for assesments and some are unable to access such institutions because of the distance. The MentalCheck website solves these issues by ensuring these therapy services can be accessed from anywhere and by anyone with an internet connection thus reducing the progression of mental illnesses as well as the charges. I developed this website in collaboration with my 3 friends. Mental Check Website
